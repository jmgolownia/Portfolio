A Distinguished Mark

Created for a distinguished author

Illustrator / Photoshop

The only task more difficult than developing your own brand is creating a logo design that expresses the essence of another person and what they do. Sandra Miranda Tully is a poet, lecturer, community spiritual leader and an all-around amazing human being. She has led an acclaimed career in speech pathology and education and is able to genuinely connect with others through speaking engagements and her literature. Sandy most recently published Every Fleeting Thing, a book of poetry for prayer and meditation, that was channeled piece by piece during a long recurrent standoff with cancer.

When Ms. Tully began to publish official copies of her book, she wanted to update the collateral sent along with book orders and handed out at speaking engagements. Although the project began as a simple request for new business cards, I suggested we look at creating an overall brand identity. A cohesive identity would create a memorable experience for her fans as well as boost her professional credentials as an author when approaching publishers for current and future works.
